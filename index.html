8. Data Stories

In the Talking Economics event regarding the "COVID Generation", it was stated by Anna that disadvantaged children suffered the worst from online learning and have had the biggest attainment gap. I tested this by using data from the Department of Education on attainment of primary school pupils and divided this by whether a child was a recipient of free school meals. As shown, there is indeed a bigger attainment gap for disadvantaged pupils compared to those who don't receive free school meals.
